ABOUT THE PROJECT

Online Payment:


Online Payment is nothing but the default in online payment fraud . In Banking sector this is an important factor to be considered.


Objective Online Payment Fraud Detection :


Find the best machine learning algorithm to help them detect fraudulent transactions on their network. Fraudsters usually hack the systems for their benefits. This project will benefit bank identify the best algorithm for the job, identify the best metrics to keep in mind and to also identify further steps they would need to take based on the model selection.

In this project, we will analyse customer-level data which has been collected and analysed during a research collaboration of Worldline and the Machine Learning Group.
